About Me

Upon graduation from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute with a degree in mechanical engineering, I began working for a large aerospace company as a project engineer, working on the Chandra space satellite. I continued to develop my project management skills working for a small firm in the petroleum industry as their construction project liaison to major oil companies.

And then I became a mother...and I left that world behind me.

Not long after, I started a web design firm. Together with a network of web professionals, I worked with small companies to establish their online presence. I ran this firm for a decade, overseeing the projects and piecing the design and the backend pieces together with HTML and CSS.

And then we moved to Texas...and I became certified to teach math.

For the next 9 years, I taught high school geometry. As a teacher I honed my organizational skills, practiced prioritizing and developed an even great amount of patience. But it was never the same sense of satisfaction or accomplishment, and I longed to be back on the web.

And then somehow things aligned, opportunites appeared... and I left teaching to go to coding bootcamp.

And here I am. I love life, I love coding.
